Our Story

A True Highland
Retreat

Nestled in the heart of Butha-Buthe, Mabida Guest House offers travellers a warm, authentic Basotho welcome. Set against the dramatic backdrop of the Maloti Mountains, our modern en-suite rooms combine comfort with the soul of the Kingdom in the Sky.

Whether you're here for business, mountain exploration, or simply a peaceful stopover on your journey through Lesotho — Mabida is your home away from home.
