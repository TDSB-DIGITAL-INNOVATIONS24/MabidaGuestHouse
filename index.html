Our Story

A True Basotho
Welcome

Nestled just 1 km from the heart of Butha-Buthe town, Mabida Guest House offers travellers a warm, authentic Basotho welcome. Our modern en-suite rooms combine comfort with the soul of the Kingdom in the Sky.

Whether you are here for business, local exploration, or a peaceful stopover — Mabida is your home away from home.
